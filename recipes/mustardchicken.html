Mustard Chicken

How to cook a delicious Mustard Chicken

Season the fillets with salt and pepper and cook in the pan with a little oil and the finely chopped chives. Keep the skillet over medium heat for a couple of minutes.

Next, remove the meat from the pan and prepare the sauce. To do this, add the mustard together with the milk cream and mix well over medium heat.

Finally, add the chicken fillets so that they are impregnated with the preparation and heated..

Ready to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 2 tablespoons of mustard
  • 1 pot of milk cream
  • 1 sprig of chives or green onion
  • 1 pinch of salt and pepper
  • 1 splash of oil
